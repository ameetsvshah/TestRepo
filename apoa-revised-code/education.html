Statement

Our Educational Program exists to improve physician competency and enhance physician performance through a comprehensive range of educational topics related to the care and treatment of patients with musculoskeletal problems. We organize yearly fellowship programs within the Asia Pacific region to enhance learning and improve outcomes for the younger surgeons in the region. The APOAs educational commitment also includes scientific meetings, operative courses and mentorship opportunities. APOA encourages any health care professional with an interest in Orthopaedic Surgery, including physicians, allied health providers and researchers to participate in its activities.
